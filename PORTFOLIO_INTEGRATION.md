## Project card (website / Notion / PDF portfolio)

**Title:** CAI — Certification-Occupant Alignment Research

**Subtitle:** Building science research · LEED, WELL, BREEAM, Fitwel

**Summary:**
Research platform measuring whether green building certifications allocate credit points toward the indoor environmental quality factors occupants actually prioritize. Analysis of 13 certification versions against 94,000+ occupant survey responses reveals systematic misalignment (average Kendall τ = −0.575), with acoustics severely under-weighted despite being occupants' top complaint.
